# Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide

**Role:** Student Digital Ambassador
**Module:** 4

## 📁 Folder Contents
This folder demonstrates the application of professional digital communication standards. It contains:

1. **`Email_Drafts.docx`**: Two sample emails showcasing proper structure, tone, subject lines, and sign-offs for academic and professional scenarios.
   * **Draft 1:** A request to a professor for an assignment deadline extension.
   * **Draft 2:** An inquiry to an internship coordinator regarding a summer internship.
2. **`social-media-checklist.md`**: A quick-reference guide featuring 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student.

*(Note: The reflection on poor digital communication and key learnings is located in the main `Project_Report` document inside the `report/` folder).*

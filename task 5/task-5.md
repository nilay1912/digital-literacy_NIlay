# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention

**Role:** Student Digital Ambassador
**Module:** 5

## 📁 Folder Contents
This folder contains resources designed to help peers recognize and avoid online threats:

1. **`casestudy.docx`**: A detailed case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud, explaining how the scam operates, who is targeted, and the consequences.
2. **`prevention-checklist.docx`**: A 'Stay Safe Online' checklist featuring 8 actionable tips for college students, with specific guidance on financial safety and official reporting channels.

*(Note: The personal reflection on this research is located in the main `Project_Report` document inside the `report/` folder).*

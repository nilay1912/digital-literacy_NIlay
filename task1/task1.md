# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

**Role:** Student Digital Ambassador
**Module:** 1

## 🖼️ Overview
This folder contains the visual deliverable for Task 1. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I created a one-page infographic designed to help my batchmates understand the core concepts of navigating the digital world safely and professionally.

**Topics Covered:**
1. What Digital Literacy Is
2. Safe Internet Practices
3. Professional Online Presence

## 🔗 Deliverables
* **Exported Image:** what_is_digital_literacy.png
* **Design Tool:** Canva

## 📝 Brief Description
This infographic was designed using Canva. It visually communicates the critical thinking required for true digital literacy, actionable steps for online security (such as utilizing 2FA and strong passwords), and the importance of building a clean, professional online footprint for future internships.

## 📝 Reflection Notes

**Platforms Chosen:** To establish a strong professional foundation, I chose to set up my digital presence on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle.

**Platform Purposes:**
* **GitHub:** This is my primary platform for version control and hosting code. It acts as a live, interactive portfolio where I can showcase my projects and technical stack (including Python, MySQL, TensorFlow, and React) to the broader developer community.
* **LinkedIn:** I am using LinkedIn as my central professional networking hub to connect with peers, professors, and industry professionals. It allows me to highlight my ongoing B.Tech education in AI & ML at VIT Bhopal and share my academic milestones.
* **Kaggle:** Because my specialization and passion lie in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Kaggle is the perfect platform to practice data science, explore massive datasets, and participate in predictive modeling competitions.

**Future Plans:**
Over the next four years, I plan to actively maintain these profiles to build a strong, interconnected digital footprint. I will regularly push my hackathon projects and coursework code to GitHub to demonstrate my practical skills. I will use LinkedIn to network with internship coordinators and share my progress, while leveraging Kaggle to continuously sharpen my machine learning capabilities. Together, these platforms will help me transition smoothly from a student to a professional AI and robotics engineer.
